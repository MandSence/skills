## Naming Conventions

### Common Prefixes

**Heaven-related**: Heaven (天), Sky (穹), Cloud (云), Star (星), Moon (月)
**Earth-related**: Earth (地), Mountain (山), River (河), Sea (海)
**Elemental**: Fire (炎/火), Water (水/霜), Wind (风/云), Earth (土), Metal (金)
**Spiritual**: Spirit (灵), Soul (魂), Heart (心), Essence (精), Qi (气)
**Power**: Divine (神), Saint (圣), Immortal (仙), Emperor (帝), King (王)
**Destruction**: Destroy (灭), Break (破), Crush (碎), Annihilate (消), Sever (斩)

### Common Suffixes

**Skills/Arts**: Art (术), Method (法), Skill (技), Technique (诀)
**Manuals**: Sutra (经), Scripture (典), Heart Method (心法)
**Movement**: Step (步), Walk (行), Dance (舞), Flight (遁)
**Weapons**: Sword (剑), Blade (刀), Spear (枪), Palm (掌), Fist (拳)

### Number Patterns

**Numbers often indicate level or power**:
- One (一), Three (三), Five (五), Seven (七), Nine (九)
- Ten (十), Hundred (百), Thousand (千), Ten Thousand (万)
- Nine usually represents highest level in xianxia

### Color Associations

**Red/Fire**: Crimson (赤), Blood (血), Flame (炎), Fire (火)
**Blue/Ice**: Azure (青), Ice (冰), Frost (霜), Cold (寒)
**Yellow/Earth**: Gold (金), Yellow (黄), Earth (土)
**White/Wind**: White (白), Silver (银), Cloud (云), Wind (风)
**Black/Dark**: Black (黑), Shadow (影), Dark (暗)
**Purple/High-level**: Purple (紫), Violet (紫) - often elite or mysterious

## Technique Quality Indicators

**Name length and complexity**:
- Simple names: Common techniques
- Complex names: Higher-level techniques
- Grandiose names: Usually powerful or sect-specific

**Adjectives indicating power**:
- Heavenly/Sky (天): Very powerful
- Divine/God (神): Immortal-level
- Spirit (灵): Standard cultivation level
- Mortal/World (凡): Basic techniques

**Special indicators**:
- Ancient (古): Lost or rare techniques
- Forbidden (禁): Dangerous techniques
- Supreme (至): Best of category
- Ultimate (极): Peak power
- Perfect (完美): Theoretical perfection
