# Wuxia Elements Reference

Detailed wuxia elements to incorporate into xianxia stories: sects, techniques, and world-building.

## Sects (门派)

### Sect Hierarchy

**Sect Leadership**:
- **Sect Master (宗主)**: Overall leader, strongest cultivator
- **Elders (长老)**: High-ranking masters, lead divisions
- **Protectors (护法)**: Elite warriors, guard important assets
- **Disciples (弟子)**: Members learning cultivation

**Disciple Ranks**:
- **Outer Disciple (外门弟子)**: New members, basic training
- **Inner Disciple (内门弟子)**: Promoted, better resources
- **Core Disciple (核心弟子)**: Elite, sect investment
- **Disciple Son/Heir (亲传弟子)**: Master's favorite, future leader

### Sect Types

**Righteous Sects (正道门派)**:
- Emphasize honor, justice, protecting mortals
- Often have strict rules and codes of conduct
- Generally respected by common people
- Examples: Heavenly Sword Sect, Cloud Pavilion

**Evil Sects (魔道门派)**:
- Ruthless methods for faster power
- Willing to sacrifice others for cultivation
- Often use forbidden or dark arts
- Examples: Blood Spirit Sect, Shadow Hall

**Neutral Sects (散修/中立门派)**:
- Balance between good and evil methods
- Focus on practical results
- Trade with all sides
- Examples: Medicine Valley, Artifact Pavilion

**Independent Cultivators (散修)**:
- Not affiliated with any sect
- More freedom but fewer resources
- Often form loose alliances
- Can be very strong through personal discovery

### Sect Territories

**Common Locations**:
- Spirit mountains (灵山): High qi concentration
- Ancient forests (古林): Spirit beasts and herbs
- Deep lakes/underground caves: Hidden sects
- Cities and towns: Sect presence and trade

**Territory Markers**:
- Formation arrays protecting boundaries
- Sect flags and insignia
- Patrols and sentries
- Spirit vein claims

## Martial Arts (武功)

### Technique Categories

**Offensive Techniques (攻击功法)**:
- Palm strikes (掌法)
- Fist techniques (拳法)
- Finger strikes (指法)
- Weapon techniques (刀法、剑法、枪法等)

**Defensive Techniques (防御功法)**:
- Body strengthening (护体神功)
- Energy shields (护罩)
- Dodge and evasion (闪避身法)
- Absorption techniques (吸功)

**Movement Techniques (身法功法)**:
- Light movement (轻功)
- Flight techniques (飞遁术)
- Teleportation (瞬移) - higher realms only
- Shadow movement (影遁)

**Auxiliary Techniques (辅助功法)**:
- Healing arts (疗伤术)
- Sense enhancement (神识强化)
- Concealment (隐匿术)
- Communication (传音术)

**Spiritual/Soul Techniques (神魂功法)**:
- Soul attacks (神魂攻击)
- Mind reading (读心术)
- Soul projection (神魂出窍)
- Mental defense (心神守护)

### Technique Levels

**Common (普通级)**:
- Basic techniques, easy to learn
- Most outer disciples learn these
- Moderate effectiveness

**Profound (玄级)**:
- Deeper understanding required
- Inner disciples and elders use
- More powerful effects

**Earth (地级)**:
- Secret techniques of sects
- Core disciples specialize
- Significant power increase

**Heaven (天级)**:
- Top techniques of cultivation world
- Only sect masters inherit
- Can influence battles significantly

**Forbidden (禁术)**:
- Dangerous or immoral techniques
- Often evil sects use these
- Great power but severe consequences

### Cultivation Manuals (修炼功法)

**Manual Quality**:
- **Human (人级)**: Basic cultivation, slow progress
- **Earth (地级)**: Good cultivation, faster progress
- **Heaven (天级)**: Excellent cultivation, exceptional results
- **Transcendent (超越级)**: Unknown or lost arts

**Manual Types**:
- **Pure yang (纯阳)**: Male cultivation, aggressive
- **Pure yin (纯阴)**: Female cultivation, gentle
- **Yin-yang balanced (阴阳平衡)**: Harmonious cultivation
- **Elemental (五行)**: Fire, water, wood, metal, earth
- **Specialized (特殊)**: Beast taming, alchemy, etc.

## Spirit Beasts (灵兽)

### Beast Ranks

**Mortal Beasts (凡兽)**:
- Regular animals with slight cultivation
- Slightly stronger than normal animals
- Often food or materials for cultivators

**Spirit Beasts (灵兽)**:
- True cultivation begins
- Can communicate through spiritual sense
- Can form contracts with cultivators

**Saint Beasts (圣兽)**:
- Equivalent to Jindan-Yuanying cultivators
- Have unique abilities
- Often guard special locations

**Divine Beasts (神兽)**:
- Equivalent to immortal realms
- Ancient bloodlines and powers
- Extremely rare

### Common Spirit Beasts

**Flying Beasts**:
- Spirit cranes (灵鹤): Speed and elegance
- Thunder eagles (雷鹰): Lightning affinity
- Cloud phoenixes (云凤): Space manipulation

**Land Beasts**:
- Spirit wolves (灵狼): Pack hunting, speed
- Iron-skinned bears (铁皮熊): Defense
- Phantom tigers (幻影虎): Illusion and stealth

**Aquatic Beasts**:
- Flood dragons (蛟龙): Water power, wisdom
- Spirit turtles (灵龟): Defense, longevity
- Jade fish (玉鱼): Swimming speed

**Special Beasts**:
- Spirit snakes (灵蛇): Poison, flexibility
- Shadow foxes (影狐): Stealth and intelligence
- Thunder monkeys (雷猴): Lightning, agility

### Beast Contracts

**Contract Types**:
- **Equal contract (平等契约)**: Both parties benefit
- **Master-servant (主仆契约)**: One dominates
- **Life-bound (生命契约)**: Lifespans linked
- **Blood contract (血契)**: Unbreakable but dangerous

**Contract Benefits**:
- Shared cultivation
- Combined abilities
- Telepathic communication
- Protection from each other

## Resources (资源)

### Spirit Stones (灵石)

**Stone Quality**:
- Low quality (下品): Common currency
- Medium quality (中品): Valuable for cultivation
- High quality (上品): Rare, used for major breakthroughs
- Top quality (极品): Extremely rare, auction items

**Usage**:
- Currency for trade
- Direct qi absorption (slow)
- Formation arrays energy
- Alchemy ingredient

### Spirit Herbs (灵草)

**Herb Categories**:
- **Medicinal herbs**: Healing and recovery
- **Cultivation herbs**: Aid breakthrough
- **Poisonous herbs**: For alchemy or weapons
- **Rare herbs**: Legendary effects

**Harvest Considerations**:
- Age affects potency (100-year vs 1000-year)
- Growth location matters (spirit vein, special soil)
- Proper harvesting techniques
- Storage methods

### Artifacts (法宝)

**Artifact Types**:
- **Attack artifacts**: Swords, spears, seals
- **Defense artifacts**: Shields, armor, barriers
- **Auxiliary artifacts**: Storage, sensing, communication
- **Formation flags**: Create arrays

**Artifact Ranks**:
- **Spirit Artifact (灵器)**: For Zhuji cultivators
- **Profound Artifact (玄器)**: For Jindan cultivators
- **Earth Artifact (地器)**: For Yuanying cultivators
- **Heaven Artifact (天器)**: For immortal realms
- **Saint Artifact (圣器)**: Legendary items

**Artifact Refinement**:
- Requires special materials
- Formation inscriptions
- Cultivator's soul imprint
- Can be upgraded with effort

## Formations (阵法)

### Formation Types

**Attack Formations**: Focus damage
**Defense Formations**: Protect areas
**Concealment Formations**: Hide locations
**Trapping Formations**: Restrict movement
**Gathering Formations**: Collect qi or resources
**Transmission Formations**: Teleportation

### Formation Components

**Flags (阵旗)**: Mobile formation elements
**Stones (阵石)**: Fixed formation anchors
**Spirit stones**: Power source
**Materials**: Special ores, beast cores

### Formation Levels

**Simple Formations**: Basic effects, easy to set up
**Complex Formations**: Multiple effects, requires expertise
**Ancient Formations**: Lost knowledge, extremely powerful
**Domain Formations**: Territory control by high cultivators

## World-building Elements

### Currency System

**Spirit Stones (灵石)**: Common currency
**Contribution Points (贡献点)**: Sect internal currency
**Favor/Status**: Social currency in cultivation world
**Techniques and Manuals**: High-value trade items

### Social Structure

**Cultivators (修仙者)**: Separate social class above mortals
**Mortals (凡人)**: Regular people, often protected
**Sects**: Organizations of cultivators
**Families**: Mortal clans with cultivators

### Geographic Features

**Spirit Mountains (灵山)**: High qi concentration, sect locations
**Spirit Forests (灵林)**: Spirit beast habitats, herb locations
**Spirit Lakes (灵湖)**: Water-element cultivation areas
**Deserted Lands (荒原)**: Dangerous, unexplored areas
**Ancient Ruins (遗迹)**: Lost inheritance locations

### Time Perception

**Cultivation Time**: Often decades or centuries
**Mortal Time**: Normal lifespan perception
**Cultivator Age**: Measured by realm, not years
- Lianqi: Like teenagers
- Zhuji: Like young adults
- Jindan: Like mature adults
- Yuanying+: Elder status regardless of actual age

### Ethics and Philosophy

**Survival First**: Cultivation world is harsh
- Weak die, strong survive
- Resources limited
- Competition constant

**Honor and Face**: Social importance
- Reputation matters
- Challenges cannot be refused lightly
- Losing face is serious

**Dao (道)**: Personal cultivation path
- Each cultivator has their own dao
- Following dao is essential
- Dao determines abilities and limits

**Karma (因果)**: Cause and effect in cultivation
- Good deeds aid cultivation
- Bad deeds create obstacles
- Some cultivate through karma manipulation
