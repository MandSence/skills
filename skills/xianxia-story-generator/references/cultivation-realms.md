# Cultivation Realms Reference

Detailed descriptions of cultivation realms from mortal to immortal in xianxia stories.

## Mortal Realms (凡人境界)

### Lianqi (练气期) - Qi Refining

**Description**: The beginning of the cultivation path. Cultivators absorb qi from nature to strengthen their bodies and meridians.

**Characteristics**:
- Slightly stronger than mortals
- Can use basic qi techniques
- Lifespan: 100-120 years
- Cannot fly (only light movement)

**Subrealms** (1-9):
- 1-3: Learn to sense qi
- 4-6: Qi circulates through major meridians
- 7-9: Qi reaches extremities, prepare for foundation

**Time to advance**: 1-10 years depending on talent

**Common techniques**:
- Qi gathering meditation
- Body strengthening exercises
- Basic elemental manipulation

---

### Zhuji (筑基期) - Foundation Establishment

**Description**: The first major milestone. Establishes a cultivation foundation that determines future potential.

**Characteristics**:
- Strength increases dramatically
- Can use qi to fly (short distances)
- Lifespan: 200-300 years
- Foundation quality affects advancement speed

**Foundation Quality**:
- **Poor (下品)**: Slow advancement, capped realms
- **Average (中品)**: Normal progression path
- **Excellent (上品)**: Fast advancement, higher potential
- **Perfect (极品)**: Exceptional talent, rare

**Common events**:
- Foundation tribulation (lightning test)
- Choosing a cultivation manual
- Joining a sect or becoming independent

---

### Jindan (金丹期) - Core Formation

**Description**: Liquid qi condenses into a solid core. Core's quality determines cultivation speed and potential.

**Characteristics**:
- Can fly freely and at high speeds
- Spiritual sense extends for kilometers
- Lifespan: 500-800 years
- Core appearance reflects cultivation attributes

**Core Quality**:
- **1-3 star**: Average cores, normal progression
- **4-6 star**: Good cores, faster cultivation
- **7-9 star**: Excellent cores, exceptional potential
- **Golden core (完美金丹)**: Perfection, legendary

**Core Attributes**:
- Pure gold: Balance of elements
- Element-colored: Specialized cultivation
- Patterned: Innate abilities or insights

---

### Yuanying (元婴期) - Nascent Soul

**Description**: The soul materializes into a nascent infant within the dantian. Major milestone separating mortals from cultivators.

**Characteristics**:
- Soul becomes independent entity
- Can possess other bodies (dangerous technique)
- Lifespan: 1,000-1,500 years
- Can survive physical body destruction (with preparation)

**Nascent Soul Abilities**:
- Independent exploration
- Soul attacks and defenses
- Body regeneration if soul intact
- Advanced spiritual sense

**Nascent Soul Tribulation**:
- Four to nine tribulations
- Lightning tests soul strength
- Failure means death or injury
- Success establishes cultivator's power

---

## Immortal Realms (仙人境界)

### Huatong (化神期) - Divine Transformation

**Description**: Soul transforms and fuses more completely with the physical body. True entry to immortal realms.

**Characteristics**:
- Can transform soul into physical form
- Space manipulation begins
- Lifespan: 2,000-3,000 years
- Can create domains (personal territories)

**Abilities**:
- Short-range teleportation
- Soul manifestation as weapon
- Domain creation and control
- Understanding of space dao

---

### Lianxu (炼虚期) - Void Refinement

**Description**: Cultivation enters the void between realms. Understanding of space deepens.

**Characteristics**:
- Long-distance teleportation
- Space storage becomes more powerful
- Lifespan: 5,000-10,000 years
- Can cross minor spatial barriers

**Abilities**:
- Space撕裂 and repair
- Void sense for spatial anomalies
- Pocket dimension creation
- Spatial dao comprehension

---

### Hecheng (合道期) - Dao Integration

**Description**: Personal dao begins to integrate with the great dao of the world.

**Characteristics**:
- Understanding of heavenly laws
- Can create minor laws within domain
- Lifespan: 20,000-50,000 years
- World rejects their presence (pressure increases)

**Abilities**:
- Law manipulation within domain
- Resistance to heavenly tribulation
- Dao imprint on world
- Immortal recognition

---

### Dacheng (大乘期) - Great Vehicle

**Description**: Almost fully integrated with dao. One step away from ascension.

**Characteristics**:
- Strong resistance to world pressure
- Can manipulate world energy on large scale
- Lifespan: 100,000+ years
- World actively seeks to expel

**Abilities**:
- Large-scale weather manipulation
- Creation of life
- Destruction on continental scale
- Near-immortal status

---

### Dujie (渡劫期) - Tribulation Crossing

**Description**: The final realm before ascension. Must survive heavenly tribulation to ascend.

**Characteristics**:
- World pressure extreme
- Tribulation clouds gather constantly
- Lifespan: Until ascension or failure
- Only one step away from true immortality

**Heavenly Tribulation**:
- Lightning tribulation tests body
- Fire tribulation tests cultivation
- Heart tribulation tests will
- Dao tribulation tests comprehension

**Success**: Ascend to immortal world
**Failure**: Death or scattered cultivation

---

## Realm Gaps (境界差距)

Each major realm creates exponential power differences:

- **Same realm, 1 subrealm difference**: 30% stronger
- **Same realm, 3 subrealms difference**: Can suppress with numbers
- **One realm higher**: Can crush lower realm easily
- **Two realms higher**: Lower realm cannot resist
- **Three+ realms higher**: Lower realm is like an ant

**Exceptional Cases**:
- Perfect foundation can challenge higher realms
- Special techniques can bridge gaps
- Teamwork can overcome realm disadvantage
- Desperation reveals hidden potential

---

## Cultivation Speed Factors

**Factors affecting speed**:
- Natural talent and spiritual roots
- Cultivation manual quality
- Spirit vein location (qi density)
- Spirit resources (stones, herbs)
- Comprehension and enlightenment
- Luck and opportunities

**Typical progression** (with good resources):
- Lianqi: 5 years
- Zhuji: 30 years
- Jindan: 100 years
- Yuanying: 500 years
- Huatong: 1,000 years
- Lianxu: 3,000 years
- Hecheng: 10,000 years
- Dacheng: 30,000 years
- Dujie: Variable (preparation for ascension)

---

## Common Cultivation Problems

**Bottlenecks (瓶颈)**:
- Realm breakthrough difficulties
- Foundation quality issues
- Comprehension limitations
- Emotional blockages (heart demons)

**Heart Demons (心魔)**:
- Inner fears and doubts
- Trauma from past experiences
- Obsession and greed
- Can prevent breakthrough

**Dao Heart (道心)**:
- Cultivator's determination and clarity
- Strong dao heart aids breakthrough
- Weak dao heart leads to failure
- Constant cultivation improves dao heart
