# Xianxia Story Generator

Generate xianxia stories combining cultivation progression systems (练气、筑基、金丹、元婴) with wuxia martial arts elements (门派、武功秘籍、江湖争斗). These stories feature cultivation to immortality, martial arts competition, and the journey of a cultivator through the realms.

## Cultivation System

### Cultivation Realms (修行境界)

The core progression system from mortal to immortal:

**Mortal Realms (凡人境界)**:
- **Lianqi (练气期)**: Initial cultivation, 1-9 subrealms
- **Zhuji (筑基期)**: Foundation establishment, 1-9 subrealms
- **Jindan (金丹期)**: Core formation, 1-9 subrealms
- **Yuanying (元婴期)**: Nascent soul formation, 1-9 subrealms

**Immortal Realms (仙人境界)**:
- **Huatong (化神期)**: Divine transformation
- **Lianxu (炼虚期)**: Void refinement
- **Hecheng (合道期)**: Dao integration
- **Dacheng (大乘期)**: Great vehicle
- **Dujie (渡劫期)**: Tribulation crossing

### Subrealm Progression

Each major realm has 9 subrealms (初期、中期、后期、大圆满):
- **Early stage (初期)**: 1-3 subrealms
- **Middle stage (中期)**: 4-6 subrealms
- **Late stage (后期)**: 7-8 subrealms
- **Perfection (大圆满)**: 9th subrealm, peak of realm

## Wuxia Elements

### Sects (门派)

Include various cultivation sects with specializations:

**Elemental Sects**:
- Fire sects: Flame arts, explosive techniques
- Water sects: Flowing arts, healing techniques
- Wind sects: Speed arts, movement techniques
- Earth sects: Defense arts, strength techniques
- Metal sects: Sharp arts, weapon techniques

**Specialized Sects**:
- Sword sects: Sword cultivation, sharp intent
- Pill sects: Alchemy, medicinal arts
- Formation sects: Array arts, defense
- Beast sects: Spirit beast taming
- Artifact sects: Weapon crafting

**Alignment**:
- Righteous sects: Honor, justice, protection
- Evil sects: Ruthless, ambitious, forbidden techniques
- Neutral sects: Balance, survival, practicality

### Martial Arts (武功)

**Technique Categories**:
- **Offensive (攻击功法)**: Strikes, blasts, weapons
- **Defensive (防御功法)**: Shields, dodges, absorption
- **Movement (身法功法)**: Speed, flight, teleportation
- **Auxiliary (辅助功法)**: Healing, senses, concealment
- **Spiritual (神魂功法)**: Soul attacks, mental arts

**Cultivation Manuals (修炼功法)**:
- Core cultivation methods for qi circulation
- Attribute-specific (fire, water, etc.)
- Quality determines cultivation speed
- Higher realms require stronger manuals

### Martial Manuals (武学秘籍)

Rare and powerful combat techniques:
- Require cultivation base to learn
- Have levels of mastery
- Often guarded by sects
- Can be inherited or discovered

## Story Elements

### Protagonist Types

Common protagonist archetypes:
- **Young talent**: Discovered with special constitution
- **Underestimated**: Ordinary appearance with hidden potential
- **Reborn cultivator**: Reincarnated with past knowledge
- **Accidental inheritor**: Finds ancient inheritance
- **Determined youth**: Overcomes lack of talent through hard work

### Antagonist Types

- **Arrogant genius**: Natural talent but cruel character
- **Elder schemer**: Powerful elder with dark agenda
- **Sect traitor**: Betrays for power or revenge
- **Demon cultivator**: Practices forbidden arts
- **Rival sect member**: Competes for resources

### Supporting Characters

- **Mentors**: Wise elders guiding protagonist
- **Friends**: Allies on the cultivation journey
- **Spirit beasts**: Powerful creature companions
- **Love interests**: Romantic subplot potential
- **Ancestors**: Past masters in spirit form

## Plot Structures

### Underdog Rise

1. Protagonist starts weak or underestimated
2. Discovers hidden ability or inheritance
3. Faces ridicule and challenges
4. Proves worth through competition or crisis
5. Gains recognition and respect
6. Continues journey toward higher realms

### Inheritance Quest

1. Ancient inheritance appears
2. Many cultivators compete for it
3. Protagonist gains advantage through character
4. Trials test more than just strength
5. Protagonist worthy of inheritance
6. New power brings new challenges

### Sect Conflict

1. Sect war or competition begins
2. Protagonist caught between sides
3. Discovers truth behind conflict
4. Makes choice based on principles
5. Fights to protect what matters
6. Gains position and authority

### Revenge Journey

1. Protagonist suffers great loss
2. Vows revenge on responsible party
3. Grows stronger through cultivation
4. Encounters allies and enemies along the way
5. Final confrontation reveals complexity
6. Resolution brings growth and wisdom
