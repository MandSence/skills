# Fairy Tale Generator

Generate Chinese-style fairy tales that blend traditional Chinese mythology, folklore, and moral lessons. These stories are suitable for children and family reading.

## Story Elements

### Core Components

Include these elements in every fairy tale:

1. **Main Character**: Usually a child, animal, or mythological creature
2. **Setting**: Ancient China, magical mountains, celestial palaces, or mystical forests
3. **Challenge**: A problem that requires courage, wisdom, or kindness to solve
4. **Helper**: A magical being, talking animal, or wise elder
5. **Resolution**: Character growth, moral lesson learned
6. **Ending**: Happy or hopeful conclusion

### Common Themes

- Courage in the face of adversity
- Wisdom and cleverness over strength
- Kindness and compassion
- Respect for nature and tradition
- The value of perseverance

## Story Structure

### Opening

Introduce the protagonist and setting clearly. Establish the world and normal situation before the conflict begins.

Example opening pattern:
> [Time] ago, in [place], there lived [character] who [unique trait].

### Development

Present the challenge or quest. Show the character's struggle and growth. Introduce helpers and magical elements naturally.

### Climax

The moment of greatest tension. The character must make a choice or overcome the final obstacle.

### Resolution

Show the aftermath of the climax. Demonstrate how the character has changed. Deliver the moral naturally.

### Ending

Provide closure and a sense of hope. End with a memorable image or thought.

## Character Types

### Protagonists

- Brave children with kind hearts
- Clever animals with human qualities
- Young apprentices learning wisdom
- Ordinary people facing extraordinary challenges

### Mythological Figures

- Dragon kings and their offspring
- Celestial maidens and gods
- Immortals and sages
- Spirit animals and magical creatures

### Helpers

- Wise old men and women
- Talking animals (cranes, turtles, rabbits, tigers)
- Spirit guides and ancestors
- Magical objects and artifacts

## Settings

### Natural Locations

- Misty mountains with ancient temples
- Deep forests with hidden paths
- Crystal lakes and waterfalls
- Bamboo groves and plum blossom gardens

### Supernatural Realms

- Celestial palaces above the clouds
- Dragon kingdoms beneath the sea
- Spirit worlds between dreams
- Ancient caves with magical treasures

## Narrative Style

### Tone

- Gentle and warm
- Respectful of tradition
- Appropriate for all ages
- Hopeful and optimistic

### Language

- Use vivid, descriptive imagery
- Include natural dialogue
- Employ repetition for rhythm
- Avoid complex or dark themes

## Output Format

Generate stories in Markdown format with the following structure:

```markdown

## [故事开始]

[故事正文...]

## [故事结局]

[结局描述...]

---

**寓意**: [故事的寓意/道德教训]
```
