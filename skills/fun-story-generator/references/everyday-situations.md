## Using These Situations

### How to Adapt

1. **Choose relatable scenario**
   - What's common experience?
   - What do people complain about?
   - What's universally annoying?

2. **Add character personality**
   - How would YOUR character react?
   - What's their specific quirk?
   - What makes their approach unique?

3. **Escalate logically**
   - Problem gets worse
   - Character makes choices
   - Each step follows previous
   - Absurdity builds naturally

4. **Find the punchline**
   - What's the funny ending?
   - What reveals the absurdity?
   - What makes reader laugh?

### Mixing Situations

Combine scenarios for richer humor:
- Technology mishap during important work meeting
- Family gathering with technology fails
- Travel disasters mixed with relationship issues
- Daily routine gone wrong leads to bigger problems

### Keeping It Fresh

Add new twists to classic situations:
- New technology creates old problems
- Pandemic-era scenarios
- Modern workplace dynamics
- Changing social norms
